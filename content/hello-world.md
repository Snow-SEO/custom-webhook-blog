There's something magical about starting a new blog. It's a blank canvas, a fresh start, a new corner of the internet to call your own.

### Why Headless?

A **headless CMS** separates the content management from the presentation layer. This means:

- You can use any frontend framework you like
- Content is served via an API or local files
- Deployment is flexible and scalable
